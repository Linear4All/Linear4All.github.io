About Us

Linear4All is being developed as the capstone project for 4 students at the University of Kentucky. Our goal is simple. To create an all-encompassing website that aids those learning Linear Algebra. All the functionality without the bad UI or overwhelming quantity of ads. Please stay tuned to see the development of our site. Here is a list of upcoming features:

  1. Matrix Addition
  2. Matrix Multiplication
  3. Matrix Row-Reduction
  4. Matrix Inverse
  5. Matrix Determinant
